🎯 Present Perfect — Quick Reference
EVER
Questions — at any time in your life
"Have you ever performed in Italy?"
NEVER
Not at any time — negative experience
"I've never forgotten a lyric."
SINCE
From a point in time until now
"...since I was fourteen."
FOR
Duration — a period of time
"...for almost ten years."
HOW LONG
Asking about duration
"How long have you lived in LA?"
JUST
Very recently — a short time ago
"I've just finished the soundcheck."
ALREADY
Sooner than expected — affirmative
"I've already released three albums."
YET
Until now — questions & negatives
"I haven't decided yet."
