Activity 1 – My Weekend Plans (pair work)3 min
Student A
Chiedi i piani del weekend: "What are you going to do this weekend?" / "Are you going to study?"

Student B
Rispondi con piani veri: "I'm going to play football and I'm going to visit my grandma." Poi scambiatevi.

Journalist
Sei un giornalista. Intervista il/la tuo/a compagno/a sui suoi piani futuri: "Are you going to learn an instrument?", "Are you going to travel this summer?", "What are you going to be when you grow up?"

Star
Rispondi come una star: "I'm going to release an album!" Fai almeno 5 domande, poi cambiate ruolo.
