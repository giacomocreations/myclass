HAVE · I / you / we / they

🎵 "Have you got a smartphone? Yes, I have!" Con I / you / we / they → have got (verde). Con he / she / it → has got (azzurro).

He's got a camera! No, he hasn't!

It hasn't got a key ring! No, it hasn't!

🗣️ Free Practice
Parla di cosa avete (e non avete) tu e i tuoi compagni.
Activity 1 – What have you got? (pair work)3 min
Student A
Chiedi: "Have you got a bike?" / "Have you got a games console?"

Student B
Rispondi sul serio: "Yes, I have." / "No, I haven't." Poi scambiatevi.
Activity 2 – Class Survey (he / she)4 min
Student A
Intervista un compagno, poi racconta alla classe in terza persona: "He has got a guitar. She hasn't got a camera."

Student B
Controlla: "Has he got a watch?" → "Yes, he has."

Solo

"I've got a pencil case. I haven't got a memory stick…"
