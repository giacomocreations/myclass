The Hot Take
Challenge

Compare, argue, defend your ranking — in English.

A is -er / more — than B
the -est / most —
A is (not) as — as B
1. You have 3 minutes to rank the 4 items as a group.
2. One spokesperson presents the ranking — full sentences only.
3. Other groups can challenge with a counter-argument.
4. Use at least 1 comparative, 1 superlative, 1 as…as.
