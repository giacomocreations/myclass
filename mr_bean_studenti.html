📌 Grammar Reminder – Present Simple: He / She / It

Most verbsadd -s  → gets, drives, parks
-sh / -ch / -x / -oadd -es  → brush → brushes
consonant + y-y → -ies  → carry → carries
irregularhave → has  /  do → does
⚠️ He brushes his teeth — not "brushs"!
