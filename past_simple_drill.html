🗣️ Free Practice – Role Play
Lavora con un compagno. Student A fa le domande, Student B risponde. Poi cambiate.
Activity 1 – Yes / No Answers2 min
Student A
Ask your partner: Did you ___ last weekend?
Use: go to the cinema / watch TV / play football / eat pizza / listen to music

Student B
Answer with Yes, I did. or No, I didn't.
Be honest — or invent a funny story! 😄
Activity 2 – Wh- Follow-up3 min
Student A
Ask: Did you go out last Saturday?
If YES → ask a follow-up: Where / When / Who with / What did you do?

Student B
Answer in full sentences. E.g. I went to the park with my friends. We played football.
Activity 3 – Detective! 🕵️5 min
Student A (Detective)
Last night there was a mystery at school 🔍
Ask your partner 5 questions to find out where they were and what they did.
Did you go to school last night? What did you do at 8pm? Did you see anyone?

Student B (Suspect)
You can tell the truth OR invent an alibi. Answer all questions using Past Simple.
Use: Yes, I did / No, I didn't / I went... / I didn't go... / I saw... / I didn't see...
